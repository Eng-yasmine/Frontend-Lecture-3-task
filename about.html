Our Story

GreenBite Catering was founded in 2018 by chef MariaLopez strong and nutritionist ames Chen. They noticed that many workplaces and schools served unhealthy, processed food — and decided to change that.

Starting from a small kitchen in Springfield, GreenBite has grown into a trusted catering company serving over 200 clients across the region.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make nutritious, delicious food accessible to everyone — at work, at school, and at home.

Our Vision

A community where healthy eating is the easy choice, not the hard one.

Our Services

GreenBite offers a range of catering solutions for every need:

  • Office Catering — Daily or weekly lunch delivery for teams of all sizes.
  • School Meals — Balanced, kid-friendly menus approved by nutritionists.
  • Event Catering — Healthy buffets for conferences, weddings, and parties.
  • Weekly Meal Plans — meals delivered to your home every week.
